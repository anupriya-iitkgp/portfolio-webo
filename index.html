About US

My name is Aryansh Singh. I am a fourth-year undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, pursuing an integrated Masters of Science in Economics (and fun).

I try trying on stuff and getting as many experiences as I can! Recently, I ended my role at the Boston Consulting Group (BCG) as a Summer Associate. Last summer, I was working in the social sphere, at Feeding India by Zomato, on strategic assignments. I have worked as a research associate at the Laboratory for Innovation Science at Harvard & the Government of India with Invest India: The National Investment Promotion and Facilitation Agency. I have previous research experience at the Indian Institute of Management, Kozhikode in the field of statistics & finance. I have also interned at the multi-billion euro German corporation, Continental AG as a finance intern in my sophomore year.

I have been involved with the Patel Hall of Residence, Entrepreneurship Cell & The Scholars' Avenue during my stay at IIT Kharagpur.

I am a Student Venture Partner at Picus Capital, an international VC Firm & would love to hear about your startup idea and discuss suitable synergies. Feel free to reach out to me here.
